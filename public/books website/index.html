Great Stephen King Novels

Some of the greatest Stephen king novels are arguably the ones from his earliest days. Starting with Carrie in 1974 moving up to 1986 with It. Some notable works in this era of Mr Kings career include:

  • Salem's lot(1975)
  • The shining(1977)
  • The Stand (1978)
  • Cujo(1981) Pet
  • Sematary(1983)

As you can see from the list, his early works include some of his best, but not only Stephen King's best but in my humble opinion, some of the best books ever written. That does not mean we should disregard his later works, a great place to start would be his latest novel, which released this month Fairy Tale.
